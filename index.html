Kentucky Colonels Ultimate

We are Colonels Ultimate. A competitive frisbee team that dedicates our time to pushing each other to be the best we can. We embrace the G.R.I.N.D. mentality every single day.

Since its inception in 2019, Colonels Ultimate has provided a competitive Open Club team to Louisville and surrounding areas. We are a passionate and dedicated group of individuals that value working hard and being an excellent teammate. Our team is comprised of mostly people from Louisville, but we also have members in Lexington, Indianapolis, and Bloomington.
